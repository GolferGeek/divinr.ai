## General
NVDA is the primary provider of accelerated computing hardware and software, positioned at the center of the global transition from general-purpose to accelerated computing architectures. The instrument operates within the semiconductor sector, specifically dominating the high-performance GPU and AI accelerator markets. Its valuation is inextricably linked to the expansion of artificial intelligence infrastructure and the capital expenditure cycles of hyperscale cloud service providers.
