The instrument's valuation is driven by its ability to scale electric vehicle (EV) production, advance autonomous driving software (FSD), and expand its energy deployment (Megapack/Solar). As a high-growth technology-adjacent stock, it is sensitive to global supply chain integrity, particularly regarding lithium-ion battery components and semiconductor availability.

Structural drivers include regulatory scrutiny of autonomous systems (Nautsa/global regulators), geopolitical tensions affecting Chinese manufacturing and demand, and the cost of capital as it influences consumer vehicle financing and large-scale infrastructure projects.
