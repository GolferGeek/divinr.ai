# Divinr.ai — Marketing Copy

## Hero

**AI market analysis you can actually understand.**

Divinr gives you a team of five specialized AI analysts — each with its own strategy, its own track record, and its own written contract you can read. No black boxes. No mystery algorithms. Just transparent, explainable signals you can inspect, challenge, and learn from.

## The Divinr Difference: Explainability Over Black Boxes

Most AI trading tools give you a number and expect you to trust it. Divinr shows you the work.

Every prediction traces back to five independent analysts with documented reasoning. Every risk score comes from a structured debate between a defender and a challenger. Every learning adaptation is visible — you can see exactly how the system got smarter and why. When the AI is wrong, you can see where it went wrong and what it changed.

This isn't about replacing your judgment. It's about giving you better inputs to make your own decisions.

## Core Features

### Multi-Analyst Predictions
Five specialized AI analysts — Macro Strategist, Technical Analyst, Sentiment Analyst, Momentum Analyst, and Fundamentals Analyst — each make an independent call with direction, confidence, and rationale. An arbitrator synthesizes them into a consensus signal, weighing confidence and track record. Every step is explainable.

### Risk Analysis & Debate
Every instrument is scored across four risk dimensions — market, fundamental, technical, and macro. A Blue Agent defends the assessment, a Red Agent challenges it with worst-case scenarios, and an Arbiter synthesizes both sides. You get the reasoning and the counterarguments, not just a number.

### Paper Trading & Portfolios
Every prediction comes with a complete trade recommendation — position size from Kelly criterion, entry/stop/take-profit levels. Watch analyst portfolios compete with equity curves benchmarked against SPY. Every position, every PnL calculation — fully transparent.

### Analyst Contracts
Each AI analyst operates under a written contract defining its philosophy, decision criteria, and known failure modes. Contracts are versioned with full audit trails — diff any two versions to see what changed and why.

### Three-Tier Learning Loop
The system learns from every mistake. Tier 1: autonomous micro-adjustments after nightly evaluations. Tier 2: a contract auditor surfaces discrepancies for admin review. Tier 3: strategic contract rewrites, tested before approval. Every adaptation is visible.

### Performance & Calibration
Track accuracy, calibration, and win rates across hundreds of predictions. Equity curves, per-instrument breakdowns, confidence-vs-outcome charts. Drill into any analyst to see exactly where it excels or struggles.

### Multi-Analyst Coordination
A correlation matrix reveals which analysts agree most often. Coverage gap analysis shows blind spots. Leave-one-out contribution scoring measures each analyst's marginal value. Transparent team dynamics.

### Real-Time Notifications
Trade recommendations, stop-loss triggers, sentiment shifts, and learning milestones — all pushed to your feed in real time. Contrarian alerts fire when a trusted analyst disagrees with your recent decisions.

### User Affinity
The platform learns which analysts match your thinking through trade decisions, challenge responses, and browsing patterns. Your dashboard personalizes around these affinities.
