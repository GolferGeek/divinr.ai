# Day Traders — Marketing Blurb

Three AI day trading strategies compete in real time: Momentum Breakout hunts for volume surges and price breaks, Mean Reversion bets on snap-backs from extreme moves, and Gap and Go captures opening gaps before the crowd. Each strategy manages its own isolated portfolio with full position tracking — entry, exit, quantity, PnL. Equity curves chart their performance against SPY. Every position closes by end of day (EOD flat), so you see clean daily results. Watch which strategy wins in different market conditions and learn the patterns that drive each approach.
