# Risk Analysis & Debate — Marketing Blurb

Before you act on any signal, understand the risk. Divinr scores every instrument across four risk dimensions — market, fundamental, technical, and macro — then stages a structured debate: a Blue Agent defends the current assessment, a Red Agent challenges it with worst-case scenarios, and an Arbiter synthesizes both sides into a final adjusted score. Every argument is visible, every adjustment is explained. You don't just get a number — you get the reasoning behind it and the counterarguments that shaped it.
