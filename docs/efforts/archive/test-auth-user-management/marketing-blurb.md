# Auth & User Management — Marketing Blurb

Every Divinr account is secured with per-user authentication and encrypted credentials, so your analysis history and virtual portfolios stay private. Invite-only beta access lets you bring teammates into Investment Learning Clubs on your terms, while role-based permissions ensure beta readers enjoy full read access without accidentally modifying shared data. From signup to trade queue, every action is gated by the permissions you've been granted — no surprises, no accidental writes.
