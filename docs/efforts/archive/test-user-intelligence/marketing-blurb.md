# User Analyst Affinity — Marketing Blurb

Divinr learns which analysts match your thinking. Every trade decision, every challenge response, every analyst page you browse builds your affinity profile — scored with exponential decay so recent interactions matter most. When an analyst you trust takes a position that contradicts your recent decisions, you get a contrarian alert: not to override your judgment, but to make sure you've seen the other side. Your dashboard personalizes around these affinities, surfacing the analysts that matter most to you first.
