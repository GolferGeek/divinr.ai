# Effort: Paid Club Tiers

## Problem

All clubs are free. There's no revenue model for the club infrastructure, no way to differentiate premium club features, and no path to monetizing the community layer.

## Intention

Introduce tiered club pricing that funds the platform while giving paying clubs meaningful advantages — more analysts, more members, priority pipeline access, advanced analytics.

## Scope

- Free tier: 1 club analyst, 20 members, basic analytics
- Pro tier ($X/mo): 5 club analysts, 100 members, full analytics, post-mortems, priority pipeline
- University tier (custom pricing): unlimited members, curriculum tools, admin dashboard, bulk invites
- Stripe integration for billing
- Upgrade/downgrade flow
- Grace period on downgrade (don't delete analysts, just disable)

## Dependencies
- Learning clubs must ship first
- Curriculum builder enhances university tier value
