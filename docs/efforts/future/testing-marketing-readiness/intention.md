# Effort: Testing & Marketing Readiness

## Problem

The system works but hasn't been comprehensively tested end-to-end, and there's no marketing material that communicates the value proposition. Before charging users, we need confidence that the full pipeline is reliable and a clear story about what makes divinr.ai different.

## Intention

Comprehensive E2E testing of the full pipeline (prediction → evaluation → learning → trading) and creation of demo scenarios and marketing copy that communicate the explainability story.

## Scope

- E2E test suite covering the full prediction lifecycle
- Demo scenarios: curated walkthroughs showing predictions, reasoning, debates, learning, and outcomes
- Marketing copy: landing page content, feature descriptions, the "why explainability matters" pitch
- Documentation of all features for user-facing help/FAQ

## Out of Scope

- Paid advertising or growth campaigns
- Pricing page implementation (that's power-user-expansion)
- Video production
